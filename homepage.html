Story of Orbitz

STORY OF ORBITZ


Originally launched in the '90s, Orbitz quickly gained a cult following. Some called it a "potable lava lamp," others just stared in awe. Its flavors were fun and fruity—like Raspberry Citrus and Pineapple Banana—and its look was out of this world. But eventually, Orbitz vanished into obscurity, like a comet that burned too bright. Years later, the brand was reborn—this time, truly embracing its cosmic identity. Welcome to the Galaxy Edition of Orbitz.
